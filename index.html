About us


We are an easy to use, efficient and fast voting system which can work on any web browser. Looking at the advancements in technology we have seen a noticeable shortfall of online voting mechanisms.
Traditional voting methods, while effective, often face challenges such as accessibility issues, long waiting times, requirement of unbiased polling officers and logistical complexities.
Our online voting system aims to address these issues, providing easily accessible and user-friendly platform that enhances voter participation and streamlines the election process and ensures unbiased results.


Objective

  • Developing an online voting system that ensures the privacy and integrity of votes. Enhancing voter participation by providing a convenient and accessible platform.
  • Reducing the administrative burden and logistical costs associated with traditional voting methods making the process of voting easy.
  • Implementing login credential system to prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  • Real-time vote counting and result generation.
